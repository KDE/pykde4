Namespace KCModuleLoader

Loads a KControl Module.

KCModuleLoader tries in several ways to locate and load a KCModule. If loading fails a zero pointer is returned. \n It is very unlikely KCModuleLoader is what you want and KCModuleProxy suits your needs.
